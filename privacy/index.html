Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

SkunkScrape provides business communication and automation tools for companies managing customer interactions through Meta platforms such as WhatsApp, Facebook, and Instagram.

Information We Collect

When users interact with a business using SkunkScrape, we may receive limited Platform Data from Meta, including:

  • Name
  • Profile picture
  • Business-scoped identifier
  • Message content

We do not collect unrelated personal data such as friend lists, interests, or advertising profiles.

How We Use Platform Data

Platform Data is used only to:

  • Display messages within a business inbox
  • Link conversations to the correct contact record
  • Prevent duplicate contact entries
  • Maintain conversation history

We do not sell, rent, or use Platform Data for advertising profiling.

Data Storage

We store only the minimum information necessary to provide messaging services. Data is protected using industry-standard security measures.

Aggregated Analytics

We may provide businesses with anonymized and aggregated metrics, such as message volume or response time. These metrics do not identify individual users.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as required to provide services to our clients or to comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

Users may contact the business they interacted with to request access, correction, or deletion of their information.
